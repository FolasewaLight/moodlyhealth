# Moodly Health

## One-line pitch
A simple daily mood companion that helps you reflect, track emotions, and build healthier habits.

## What it does
Moodly Health lets people log how they feel, spot triggers and patterns, and turn insight into action. It uses gentle prompts inspired by CBT to help reframe negative thoughts, suggests quick self-care actions, and can generate a weekly summary you may share with a counselor.

## Who it is for
- Anyone who wants an easy way to understand daily emotions
- Students and young professionals building healthy routines
- People in therapy who want simple context between sessions

## Why it matters
Many people struggle to name feelings or notice patterns. Moodly Health turns small daily check-ins into clear insights and gentle actions that support emotional balance and long term growth.

## Core features
- Reflect: short guided prompts inspired by CBT
- Track: fast mood check-ins with notes and color-coded trends
- Connect: optional Support Circles to share a brief status with trusted people
- Act: suggested micro actions like breathing, journaling, short walks
- Grow: streaks, milestones, and a visual Mood Spectrum
- Support: weekly summaries for personal use or therapy

## How it works at a glance
1. Log a mood in under 30 seconds  
2. See patterns and triggers over time  
3. Get small, relevant actions to try today

## Tech at a glance
- Frontend: React or Next.js
- Backend: Node.js with Express
- Database: PostgreSQL via Prisma ORM
- Auth: JWT session tokens
- Hosting: Vercel for frontend, Render or Railway for backend, Neon or Supabase for Postgres
